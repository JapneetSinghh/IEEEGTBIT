    IEEE GTBIT

    The goal of IEEE GTBIT SB is to transform students into polished working professionals that are industry-tailored. Expanding the knowledge pool of students and giving them practical exposure is one of our top priorities along with many others like communication skills. We provide them with the proper tools, guidance and support system which they need in their journey to climb the staircase of success. Our mentors along with the team are aimed at providing an environment that helps the students to become better each day.
