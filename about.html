    About IEEE

    IEEE stands for Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers and is an organization that has been true to its name and motto since its establishment in 1963. It focuses on increasing the technical aptitude of students and working professionals worldwide across electrical systems, electronics, computer science, technical fields, and telecommunication, etc. Itâ€™s a USA based organization with its headquarters located in Piscataway, New Jersey, the USA that has become one of the largest international organizations for engineers and people related to the various fields of engineering worldwide.

    About IEEE GTBIT

    IEEE GTBIT SB(Student Branch) is a committed and ambitious subunit of IEEE. IEEE GTBIT SB helps students to sharpen skills by organizing events, workshops, etc. We also make students aware of the offers, events, facilities provided to them via IEEE. Our student branch has helped students by extensive methods throughout all these years by honing their technical skills, making them better engineers and overall professionals by increasing their interest in engineering with resources. We also successfully managed to improve their social, marketing, and communication skills. Our esteemed branch was first established in 2008 and is flourishing ever since.

    Our Mission

    The goal of IEEE GTBIT SB is to transform students into better working professionals that are industry-tailored. Expanding the knowledge pool of students and giving them practical exposure is one of our top priorities also many others like group discussion and communication skills. Our mentors connect with various other colleges under the IEEE banner that helps give students more exposure and talk to real-life industry experts. Our branch helps each member improve their weak spots through workshops organized for technical skills, social skills, and more. WIE chapter has been an integral part of our society. Hence we are also committed to empowering women in engineering and expanding the list of female engineers.


    Our Vision

    Our vision is to close the gap between the teaching done in classrooms and actual professional work by implementing creative and even a few unorthodox learning methods for more practically inclined learning.


    Message from our Branch Counselor

    Mukesh Sahu

    IEEE GTBIT has been helping students to thrive in a professional work environment for years. Being the Branch Counselor of IEEE GTBIT gives me immense pleasure and satisfaction as a teacher. The students involved with our branch value ethics, honesty, and truthful learning. Over the years, we have also opened separate CS and WIE chapters which have helped broaden the spectrum of our children.

    The events that we conduct are designed to meet the demands of today's competitive world. The students with us are taught, practical skills which will help them flourish when they step out in the industrial world. Our goal is to create an efficient system within the society that is both challenging and inspiring. We work with students to help them improve their academic skills, technical skills, human values, and decision-making abilities.

    Students are encouraged to be competent communicators with strong active listening skills through numerous activities. During our active hours, they demonstrate teamwork, respect for one another, and appreciation for each other's work. I believe it is my duty to establish the culture in our society, and I collaborate closely with other IEEE mentors and our students to ensure that we have the opportunity to demonstrate these characteristics.
